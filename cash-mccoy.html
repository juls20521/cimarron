Personality

  • Silver-Tongued: Cash can talk his way into a high-stakes game or out of a hanging with equal ease; he treats conversation like a chess match where the goal is to keep everyone smiling.
  • Perceptive: Years at the card table have given him an uncanny ability to read people, noticing the slight sweat on a brow or the twitch of a finger that betrays a lie.
  • Compulsively Amiable: He uses his "Easy-Going Owen" charm as a tactical shield, believing that if people are laughing at his jokes, they aren't looking for the extra card up his sleeve.
  • Risk-Addicted: Whether it’s a bad poker hand or a narrow escape from a posse, he thrives on the "rush" and feels most alive when the odds are stacked heavily against him.
  • Morally Flexible: He isn't a "bad" man, but he’s a pragmatist; he’ll steal from a greedy railroad tycoon without a second thought, though he’d never take a dime from a widow.
  • Rootless: Having lost his family and his home to the border wars, he views any form of permanent attachment as a cage he can't afford to be in.

Motivations & Disposition
